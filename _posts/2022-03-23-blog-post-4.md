Attached are transcriptions for the recent Supreme Court confirmation hearing of Judge Ketanji Brown Jackson (D.C. Circuit), which are available in (.rdata) format. It was constructed using C-SPAN's (rough) closed captioning transcriptions of the hearing's proceedings, which I was able to access by referencing the relevant hyperlink for each portion of the hearing and converting it to a plain text (.txt) file. 

As a disclaimer, I should note that these transcripts might have a few errors. The closed captioning software employed by C-SPAN proved to misspronounce terms (or names) or omit certain rhetoric all together -- especially when the committee members were squabbling (which was fairly often). Nonetheless, it is my hope that this data serves as a placeholder until official transcripts are released by the Senate Judiciary Committee. 
Please feel free to use this data at your own discretion. All I ask is that you provide the necessary citation for C-SPAN and for myself having collected and sorted the transcription data.
